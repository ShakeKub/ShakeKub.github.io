Blackjack Guide & Strategy

üéØ What is Blackjack?

Goal: Get cards totaling as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).

Blackjack: An Ace + any 10-value card (10, J, Q, K) = 21 points

üÉè Card Values

  • Ace (A): 1 or 11 points (whichever is better)
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points each
  • Number cards (2-10): Face value

üìã Basic Actions

  • Hit: Take another card to increase your total
  • Stand: Keep your current hand (no more cards)
  • Double: Double your bet and take exactly one more card
  • Split: Split identical cards into two separate hands
